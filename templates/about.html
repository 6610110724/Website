About All Tactics



All Tactics is a web-based educational football simulation that allows you to play football while learning about football tactics. By learning the All Tactics, you will be able to create your own style and football tactics, as well as level up your player while improving your football knowledge, which can be applied in the real world to help you enhance your football skills.
